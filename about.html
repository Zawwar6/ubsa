About UBSA

Discover who we are, what we stand for, and how we support Bangladeshi undergraduate students at the University of Saskatchewan. UBSA is committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment where students can connect, celebrate their cultural identity, and thrive both academically and socially throughout their university journey.

Who We Are

The University of Saskatchewan Bangladeshi Students Association (UBSA) is a student-led organization dedicated to representing and supporting Bangladeshi undergraduate students on campus.

We aim to create a welcoming environment where students can connect, celebrate their cultural identity, and grow academically, socially, and professionally throughout their university journey.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To foster unity, cultural pride, and student success by organizing inclusive events, providing support networks, and creating opportunities for leadership and engagement.

Our Vision

To build a strong, connected Bangladeshi student community that actively contributes to campus life while preserving and celebrating Bangladeshi heritage.

Our Values

To build a strong, connected Bangladeshi student community that actively contributes to campus life while preserving and celebrating Bangladeshi heritage.
