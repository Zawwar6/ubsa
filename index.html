Who We Are

UBSA is a student-led organization representing Bangladeshi undergraduate students at the University of Saskatchewan. Our mission is to build a welcoming and inclusive community where students feel connected, supported, and proud of their cultural identity.

Through cultural celebrations, social gatherings, academic support initiatives, and collaborative events, UBSA creates opportunities for students to engage, grow, and lead. We strive to preserve Bangladeshi traditions while fostering meaningful connections within the wider university community.
