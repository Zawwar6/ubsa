UBSA Events

Explore the vibrant events organized by the University of Saskatchewan Bangladeshi Students Association (UBSA). From cultural festivals to academic workshops, our events are designed to celebrate Bangladeshi heritage, foster community spirit, and support the personal and professional growth of our members. Join us in creating memorable experiences that enrich student life on campus.

Cultural Festivals

Celebrate the rich traditions of Bangladesh with our annual cultural festivals. Enjoy traditional music, dance, food, and art that showcase the vibrant heritage of our community.

Academic Workshops

Enhance your skills and knowledge through our academic workshops. Topics range from study techniques to career development, all aimed at supporting your success at the University of Saskatchewan.

Social Gatherings

Build lasting friendships and connections through our social gatherings. From casual meet-ups to themed parties, these events provide a relaxed environment for members to unwind and socialize.

Community Service

Give back to the community with our organized service events. Participate in volunteer opportunities that make a positive impact while fostering a sense of unity among members.
